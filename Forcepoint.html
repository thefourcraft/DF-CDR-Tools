Zero Trust CDR

Forcepoint's Zero Trust CDR is an innovative technology that combines Zero Trust principles and data protection to create a new level of data security. Unlike traditional methods focused on malware detection, Zero Trust CDR starts with the assumption that nothing can be trusted. It extracts verified business data from files and constructs new files for secure transmission. This approach provides threat-free, pixel-perfect, and usable files, countering advanced zero-day attacks effectively. Unlike conventional methods, it remains resilient even against subtle malware alterations, reducing costs and easing the load on SOC teams. Forcepoint extends the technology to email security, cleaning email content proactively, and also offers an early access program to counter email-borne malware. By incorporating Zero Trust CDR into Data Loss Prevention (DLP), organizations can greatly enhance their data security against sophisticated threats.
