Votiro

Votiro safeguards businesses from malware using Positive Selection Technology, a form of Content Disarm and Reconstruction (CDR). It removes threats from files and reconstructs them with safe content, ensuring complete security. Their service, Votiro Cloud, integrates with email, web browsers, and more for secure content delivery.

Votiro Cloud

Votiro Cloud is a Layer 7, Open API-based service that provides zero trust content security. It seamlessly integrates with existing IT and Security platforms, delivers safe content through various channels, and utilizes Positive Selection Technology to ensure the safety of files entering your organization. Votiro Cloud follows a zero trust approach, offers ease of use and integration, and has achieved SOC 2 Type II Compliance for comprehensive security
