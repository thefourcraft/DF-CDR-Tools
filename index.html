Content Disarm Reconstruction

The next generation of digital document transfer security

WHY CDR?

Content Disarm and Reconstruction (CDR) technology is a crucial cybersecurity measure that disarms malicious files while preserving content integrity, protecting against evolving threats like zero-day exploits and advanced malware. Its proactive approach goes beyond traditional antivirus solutions, making it an essential component in today's digital landscape.

Odi-x is a cybersecurity company that specializes in Content Disarm and Reconstruction (CDR). Their patented technology is designed to disarm malicious code from files. Instead of trying to detect malware, odi-x generates a malware-free copy of the file for the user. This approach provides total protection from known and unknown threats delivered to corporate networks by incoming files.
